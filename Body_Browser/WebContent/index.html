Human Anatomy

"Explore the human body like never before! Anatomy is the study of the shape and form of the human body. The anatomy is determined by a strong skeleton made of bone and cartilage, surrounded by fat, muscle, connective tissue, organs, and other structures which are all important for proper functioning of a human body. We, BlackHawks have made an attempt to create an interactive visual display application to allow users to explore the human body in 3-D"
